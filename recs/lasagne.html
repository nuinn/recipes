Lasagne

Lasagne is a traditional Italian dish containing layers of flat pasta separated by a meat sauce filling, "ragu", and covered with a cheesy bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • lasagne sheets

Ragu sauce

  • an onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, thinly sliced
  • 500g pork mince
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 cups wine
  • 100g tomate puree
  • 1l passata
  • a can of chopped tomatoes

Bechamel sauce

  • 750ml whole fat milk
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 100g plain flour
  • 100g parmesan
